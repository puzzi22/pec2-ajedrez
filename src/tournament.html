Poblenou Chess Club
Tournament 2023

Tournament Rules

This exciting event brings together chess enthusiasts from all skill levels to celebrate the love of the game. Over two thrilling days, competitors will engage in intense matches to determine the ultimate chess champion. Expect a friendly atmosphere, fierce competition, and the chance to make new friends who share your passion for chess. Don't miss this incredible opportunity to showcase your skills and connect with the local chess community.

  1. The tournament is open to players of all ages and skill levels residing in the Poblenou neighborhood.
  2. Participants must register for the tournament by submitting their full name, contact information, and a brief summary of their chess experience through the online registration form. The form will be online soon.
  3. Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis, with a limited number of spots available. Early registration is highly encouraged to secure a place in the competition.
  4. A nominal registration fee of €10 is required to confirm your participation. Payment details will be provided upon submission of the registration form.
  5. Participants must adhere to the tournament schedule and rules, which will be provided to all registered players before the event.

Master the Board: Triumph & Treasure Awaits!

The 2023 champion of the Poblenou Chess Club Tournament will not only earn the prestigious title but also receive a prize. The winner will be awarded prize of 200€, a complimentary membership to the Poblenou Chess Club for the next five years, and a beautiful, high-quality chess set. This prize serves as both a reward for their exceptional skill and an incentive to help developing the passion for chess within our vibrant community.
