Chess Tournament 2023

The Poblenou Chess Club

Welcoming enthusiasts of all skill levels, the Poblenou Chess Club offers a space to indulge in the game of kings. Members enjoy the freedom to choose opponents, with occasional skill-based matches. Providing boards, clocks, and pieces, we ensure a seamless gaming experience. Each September, our neighborhood tournament crowns a local champion. Join us for engaging matches, friendly competition, and connection with fellow chess aficionados.
