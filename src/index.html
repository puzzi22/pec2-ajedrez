Chess Open 2023

The Poblenou Chess Club

The Poblenou Chess Club welcomes enthusiasts of all skill levels and experience to indulge in the game of kings. Our members enjoy the freedom to choose their opponents, although on occasion, matches are organized based on players' skills. We take pride in providing all the essentials - boards, clocks, and pieces - for a seamless gaming experience. Each year in September, we host an exciting neighborhood tournament that culminates in the crowning of the local champion. Join us at the Poblenou Chess Club for engaging matches, friendly competition, and the chance to connect with fellow chess aficionados.
